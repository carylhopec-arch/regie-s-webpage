My Autobiography

By Regie Villamor Magnifico

Introduction

Hello! I’m Regie Villamor Magnifico, and this is my autobiography. I was born on October 4, 2003, in Antipolo City. Throughout my journey so far, I’ve encountered many experiences that have helped shape me into the person I am today.

Early Life

I was born into a close-knit family and spent my early years in an environment that shaped my values, curiosity, and resilience. Growing up, I learned important lessons through everyday experiences, from family traditions to the challenges that helped me mature.

Education

I began my educational journey at Bagong Nayon 1 Elementary School. I continued at Antipolo National High School and later attended Our Lady of Fatima University for senior high. I am currently pursuing my college education at Marikina Polytechnic College.

Career

My professional journey began at EMParan Engineering Services as a Junior Engineer, where I gained hands-on experience in Civil Technology projects and developed strong technical and problem-solving skills.

Personal Life

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling, hanging out with friends, drinking coffee (especially matcha), playing online games, and reading books. These activities help me maintain balance and happiness.

Future Goals

My goal is to become a licensed engineer and live a stable and fulfilling life—financially, emotionally, and physically—while contributing positively to my community.
